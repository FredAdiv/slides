## Turle Commands
{id: turle-commands}

* [docs](https://docs.python.org/library/turtle.html)


* forward(distance), fd
* backward(distance), bk
* left(angle),  lt
* right(angle),  rt
* goto(x, y)
* home()      goto(0,0)   - the center
* pos()      - get current position
* penup()
* pendown()
* clear()
* circle(radius), circle(radius, extent, steps)
